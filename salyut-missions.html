Introduction

Missions

Components Spacecraft

A mission to a Salyut station includes a spacecraft launch from earth, attaining orbit, docking with the station and return to Earth for crewed spacecraft or deliberate destruction by a controlled re-entry for cargo craft. The duration of a mission varies, depending on its purpose.

Salyut Missions were named after the spacecraft used for crew transport.

Soyuz and Soyuz T spacecraft were used for crew transport and Progress spacecraft were mainly used for logistic supplies.

The Soyuz craft were also used as emergency escape vehicles. Therefore at least one Soyuz was always docked at the station during crew presence.
